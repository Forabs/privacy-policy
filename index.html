Privacy Policy for Senior Camera

Last updated: April 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Senior Camera ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the App.

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Access

Senior Camera is a camera app designed for taking photos, recording videos, viewing captured content, and unlocking premium features through in-app purchases.

To provide these features, the App may access the following:

  • Camera permission, to take photos and record videos
  • Microphone permission, to record audio with videos
  • Photo Library add-only permission, to save photos and videos to your device's photo library

These permissions are requested by iOS and can be granted or denied by you at any time in your device settings.

2. Photos and Videos

When you use the App to take photos or record videos, that content is processed locally on your device.

If you choose to save a photo or video, the App writes it to your device's Photo Library.

We do not upload your photos or videos to our own servers for storage or processing.

3. In-App Purchases

The App offers premium features through Apple's in-app purchase system.

When you purchase, restore, or verify a subscription or lifetime purchase, the App may receive limited purchase-related information from Apple, such as:

  • whether a purchase exists
  • whether a subscription is active
  • whether a restore request succeeded

Payments are processed by Apple. We do not receive your full payment card details, Apple ID password, or other sensitive billing credentials.

4. How We Use Information

  • enable photo capture and video recording
  • enable audio recording for videos
  • save photos and videos to your Photo Library
  • determine premium entitlement status
  • unlock paid features
  • restore prior purchases
  • maintain basic app functionality

5. What We Do Not Do

Except where required by law or with your consent, we do not:

  • create user accounts
  • collect your name, phone number, email address, contacts, messages, or precise location through the App
  • upload your photos or videos to our own servers
  • sell or rent your personal information
  • use cross-app or cross-site tracking for advertising purposes

6. Third-Party Services

The App relies on Apple-provided frameworks and services, including:

  • AVFoundation for camera and video functionality
  • Photos for saving content to the Photo Library
  • StoreKit and the App Store for in-app purchases, subscription management, purchase restoration, and entitlement verification

Your use of these Apple services may also be subject to Apple's own privacy policy and terms.

7. Data Storage and Security

  • Photos and videos created with the App are stored locally on your device or in your Photo Library when you choose to save them.
  • Temporary video files may be created locally on your device as part of the recording workflow.
  • Purchase status is checked using Apple's systems.

We take reasonable steps to rely on platform-provided safeguards, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for general audiences.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through our own servers. If you believe a child has provided information in a way that raises a privacy concern, please contact us.

9. Your Choices

  • deny or revoke camera, microphone, or Photo Library permissions in iOS Settings
  • manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple account settings
  • restore eligible purchases using the App's restore purchase feature
  • stop using the App at any time

Please note that if you deny certain permissions, some App features may not function properly.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App, legal requirements, or business operations.

If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date and, where appropriate, provide additional notice.
